What is Ecocide?

Ecocide is the large-scale destruction of the natural world. It includes actions like deforestation, oil spills, and pollution that harm ecosystems and people.

Case Studies

  • Amazon Deforestation: Millions of hectares lost yearly.
  • Deepwater Horizon Oil Spill: Marine ecosystems devastated.
  • Local Example (NZ): Waikato River pollution from industry runoff.
